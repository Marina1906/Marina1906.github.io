About

Elia (M) has 8 years of teaching experience in different language training schools and kindergartens. Last 2 years she works like ESL online tutor. Teaching is her passion and profession as well. In her classes she uses a lot of props and applies different teaching methods. Her goal is to awake children's interest for foreigin language and education and to boost their confidence to speak. In her classes pupils are not only passive listeners, but active participants in learning process. Classes are vivid and interactive. The importance of early childhood education was approved by many famous educators. Her favorite educator is Maria Montessori and after almost nine years of teaching experience she can only say 'Free the child's potential and you will transform him into the world'.🌷
